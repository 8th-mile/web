Mini Cricket

General Rules

  1. Match will be of 5 overs per innings
  2. Proper overarm bowling action is mandatory and the same will be verified, if found improper free hit will be given
  3. Every team should get minimum of 1 bat of their own
  4. Balls will be provided only for the matches
  5. Umpires decision will be final
  6. Teams should report at the venue at the given reporting time
  7. Extras will be counted except for leg byes
  8. No free hits for no balls
  9. No warnings will be given for no balls(bouncers also)
  10. Two mandatory powerplays will be there in an innings one each per bating and bowling side
  11. Batting powerplay
    1. Should be taken by the batting team in any of the last two overs
    2. Runs scored in this over will be doubled
  12. Bowling powerplay
    1. Should be taken by the bowling team in any of the first two overs
    2. Only three fielders are allowed
    3. The scoring area will either be on the OFF side or ON side according to the choice of the bowling team

Registration fee: 500/team

Requirements
